# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: June 2025_

This Cookie Policy explains how this site uses cookies and similar technologies, in accordance with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and the **ePrivacy Directive**.

This policy should be read together with our [Privacy Policy].

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to website owners.

Cookies may be:
- **Session cookies**, which are deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies**, which remain on your device until they expire or are deleted

---

## 2. Legal Basis for Using Cookies

We use cookies on the following legal bases:

- **Strictly necessary cookies:**  
  Processed on the basis of our **legitimate interest** (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), as they are essential for the operation of the Website.

- **All other cookies (functional, analytics, marketing):**  
  Processed **only with your consent** (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

You may withdraw your consent at any time (see Section 7).

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the Website to function and cannot be disabled. They are required for:
- Secure website operation
- Booking and checkout processes
- Fraud prevention and security

These cookies do **not** require consent.

---

### 3.2 Functional Cookies
Functional cookies allow the Website to remember your choices (such as language or booking preferences) to provide enhanced functionality.

These cookies are only placed **with your consent**.

## 6. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our Website, you are presented with a cookie banner that allows you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Choose your cookie preferences by category

Non-essential cookies are **not placed unless you give your consent**.
